ConnectCraft - Privacy Policy

Last updated: June 18, 2025

1. Information We Collect

The ConnectCraft - LinkedIn Message Automation extension ("the Extension") helps you automate and personalize your LinkedIn connection requests. To do this, the Extension collects the following types of information:

  • User Activity: We observe your interactions within the Extension's popup (like saving your custom message templates and settings). We also interact with the LinkedIn page to read specific information.
  • Website Content: The Extension reads publicly available information directly from LinkedIn pages, specifically:
    • The first name of the LinkedIn member you are inviting (from their profile page).
    • Your profile information when needed for message personalization.

2. How We Use Your Information

The information collected by ConnectCraft is used only to provide the core features of the Extension:

  • To personalize your LinkedIn invitation messages by automatically inserting the member's first name and other relevant details into your message templates.
  • To save your custom message templates and preferences within your browser, so your settings are remembered for future use.
  • To provide smart name detection and template management functionality.

3. Information Storage and Sharing

  • All collected data (your custom message templates, settings, and the names read from LinkedIn pages) is stored locally within your browser.
  • We do not send, sell, or share any user data with outside parties.
  • We do not use or share user data for purposes unrelated to the main function of the Extension.
  • We do not use or share user data to determine creditworthiness or for lending purposes.
  • No data is transmitted to external servers or third-party services.

4. Data Security

Your privacy and data security are important to us:

  • All data processing happens locally in your browser
  • No external data transmission occurs
  • You can delete all stored data by uninstalling the extension

5. Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update our Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any changes by updating the "Last updated" date at the top of this Privacy Policy.
